About Bodh Gaya

Bodh Gaya is a village in northeast indian state of bihar.
consist one of the most important buddhist pilgrimage sites.
It is the birthplace of buddhism and the place where gautam buddha attained enlightenment.

Historical and cultural Significance

Bodh Gaya also has historical and cultural importance,serving as a gateway to various buddhist sites and rich tapestry of religious and cultural traditions.

  • Enlightment Sites
  • Origin of Buddhism
  • Mahabodhi Temple

Tourist place

Some famous tourist places in Bodh Gaya :

  • Lotus Tank
  • Buddha Kund
  • Statue of the Buddha
  • Tibetan Monastery
