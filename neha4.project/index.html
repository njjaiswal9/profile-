About Bodh Gaya

Bodh Gaya is a serene village in Bihar, India, renowned as the place where Gautam Buddha attained enlightenment. It is a vibrant center for Buddhist pilgrimage, spiritual learning, and cultural heritage.

Historical & Cultural Significance

  • Enlightenment Sites
  • Origin of Buddhism
  • Mahabodhi Temple (UNESCO World Heritage)

Tourist Attractions

  • Lotus Tank
  • Buddha Kund
  • Statue of the Buddha
  • Tibetan Monastery

Plan Your Visit

Experience meditation retreats, explore ancient temples, and immerse yourself in the peaceful ambiance of Bodh Gaya. Whether you seek spiritual growth or cultural exploration, Bodh Gaya welcomes you.
